About Us

Mastering True Music is an audio mixing and mastering service that works to ensure your mixes or tracks have a good tonal balance, overall control of dynamics, stereo image, optimal final volume according to the current industry standards, and ultimately, control over the quality of the final result of your track.

To achieve this, we have a proper combination of analog equipment (hardware) and state-of-the-art software that will give your project the touch it needs to highlight the qualities of your music.
